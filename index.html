Evaluation Criteria

How we score each opportunity across 4 key dimensions:

Market Size

How much money is in play? Total addressable market and transaction volume.

Example: Liquidation = High ($2B+ distressed assets), Platform pricing = Medium (niche markets)

Information Asymmetry

How big is the information gap we're exploiting? Stronger asymmetry = better arbitrage.

Example: FSBO groups = High (sellers don't know buyer networks), VC claims = Medium (verifiable but time-consuming)

Technical Risk

How hard is this to build? Technical complexity and execution difficulty.

Example: Price monitoring = Low (web scraping), VC fact-checking = High (complex AI reasoning)

Time to Market

How quickly can we validate and launch? Speed to revenue generation.

Example: Platform arbitrage = 3-6 months, Professional networks = 12+ months

Arbitrage Patterns

The fundamental patterns we look for in information arbitrage:

Speed Arbitrage

Acting faster than humans can process information.

Platform pricing alerts, liquidation notifications

Scale Arbitrage

Network effects and reaching more parties simultaneously.

1 seller → 1000 buyers vs human's ~10

Geographic Arbitrage

Exploiting location-based cost or access differences.

FSBO groups (local) → global buyer networks

Expertise Arbitrage

Capturing value from specialized knowledge gaps.

German financial analysts → FinTech AI companies

Validation Framework

Phase 1: Market Reality Check

Do people actually negotiate these things?
Is there real money on the table?
Are current solutions inadequate?

Phase 2: Technical Feasibility

Can we access the data needed for intelligent negotiation?
How standardized are the negotiation processes?
What legal/compliance constraints exist?

Phase 3: Business Model Viability

How do we reach decision-makers?
Customer acquisition cost vs. lifetime value?
What defensible advantages can we build?

Phase 4: Scaling & Network Effects

Does more usage create better outcomes?
Can we bring both buyers and sellers onto platform?
Winner-take-most dynamics potential?
