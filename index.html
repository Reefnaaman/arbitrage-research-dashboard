Validation Framework

Phase 1: Market Reality Check

Do people actually negotiate these things?
Is there real money on the table?
Are current solutions inadequate?

Phase 2: Technical Feasibility

Can we access the data needed for intelligent negotiation?
How standardized are the negotiation processes?
What legal/compliance constraints exist?

Phase 3: Business Model Viability

How do we reach decision-makers?
Customer acquisition cost vs. lifetime value?
What defensible advantages can we build?

Phase 4: Scaling & Network Effects

Does more usage create better outcomes?
Can we bring both buyers and sellers onto platform?
Winner-take-most dynamics potential?
